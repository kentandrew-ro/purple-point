Forgot Your Password?

For your account's security, passwords cannot currently be reset automatically.

To request a password change:

  1. Contact the clinic directly or visit the clinic reception.
  2. Provide your full name, registered email address, and contact number.
  3. Clinic staff will verify your identity and forward the request to the authorized administrator.
  4. Once approved, you will receive a temporary password or be assisted in creating a new password.
  5. Sign in using the temporary password and change it immediately.

Your new password must contain:

  • At least 8 characters
  • At least one uppercase letter
  • At least one special character

Never share your password or temporary password with anyone.
